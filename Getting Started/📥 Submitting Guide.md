# Submitting Assignments

Each unit there is a required **HackerRank Assessment** to submit. These required HackerRank assessments take about 60-90 minutes and involve several multiple-choice or coding questions to be completed. We will send your score to you each week to help you understand your progress at each point throughout the course.

You **must complete these HackerRanks by the deadline each week** in order to remain a participant in the course. Check out the [HackerRank FAQs](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/hackerrank_faq "HackerRank FAQs")for more information.

> 📣 **Note:** Your HackerRank Assessment can only be submitted once, and cannot be edited after submission. Please double-check your work before submitting!

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/submission_guide#heading-how-to-submit-assignments)How to Submit Assignments

We **automatically scan your HackerRanks so you don't have to do anything except complete the assessment directly on HackerRank** through the links we provide and with the email matching the ones associated with this course.

Sometimes our system can get confused if you provided an alternate email for your HackerRank test. To avoid any issues with your submissions, make sure that when taking the HackerRank tests, you **always provide the same email address there each time that you have registered with us for the course**.

If you used an alternative email address, or otherwise suspect that there may be issues or you aren't sure if your submissions are being recorded properly, **please reach out to us via [support@codepath.org](mailto:support@codepath.org)** so we can make sure everything is linked up properly on our side.
