## Unit 06: Graphs III & Linked Lists II

Welcome to Unit 6!

#### Lecture Session

In lecture, you will explore advanced graph algorithms such as Dijkstra's and Union Find to solve more complex connectivity and shortest path problems.

In this lecture, you will...

-   Apply Dijkstra's algorithm to solve shortest path problems
-   Use Union Find to efficiently manage connected components

#### Breakout Session & HackerRank

In this unit, you will continue to build your skills traversing and manipulating linked lists. You will solve problems involving linked list variations including circularly linked lists, doubly linked lists, and linked lists with cycles. This unit will also cover advanced algorithmic techniques for linked lists including creating a temporary head node, performing multiple passes of a linked list, and detecting cycles in a linked list using a variation of the two-pointer technique.

In the problem sets, you will...

-   Solve problems involving singly, doubly, and circularly linked lists
-   Use the slow and fast pointer method to detect cycles in a linked list
-   Become familiar with creating a temporary head node to solve problems that require manipulating a linked list's head node
-   Solve problems requiring multiple traversals of a linked list
-   Evaluate the time and space complexity of problems involving linked lists
